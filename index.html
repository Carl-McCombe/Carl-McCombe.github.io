Thesis Submission!!!

I just submitted my PhD Thesis for examination!

  • 17 November 2023

After 3 and a half years of interesing, exciting, and sometimes, exhausting research, I have submitted my PhD thesis. Entitled 'Functional characterisation of Nudix hydrolase effectors from phytopathogenic fungi', this extensive body of work provides the most comprehensive characterisation of a class of enzymatic fungal effectors to date. My research has resulted in two major first-author publications, one in New Phytologist, and the other is in review and available as a preprint.
